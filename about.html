Bacteria brought life to earth, we bring life to virtual reality

Awowd virtual world of Earth is a space that envelops earth topography, local ecology, weather simulation and history, and changes over time as people interacts with this space.

Our primary goal is to provide the most realistic content so that we may create simulations that are both detailed and extremely focused.

We hope to capture the mesmerizing quality of a deep yet unexpected connection to the natural world, and channel this into virtual world. To do this, we're synthesizing artificial intelligence, simulations, and art to populate virtual reality with creatures and environments that are sentient, evolving, and variable.

Our goal is not only to bring enjoyment to our users, but also to give way to the possibility of further experimentation, knowledge acquisition, and interactions with the world.
